About Us

Welcome to Fitness Freaks, where we are more than just a brand - we are a community of fitness enthusiasts united by our passion for an active and healthy lifestyle. Our mission is to empower individuals like you to reach their fitness goals, no matter where you start or where you're headed. At Fitness Freaks, we believe that together, we can go further.

Our Journey

Our story began in 2023, when three fitness enthusiasts, Aryan, Ahmad, and Rapinder, shared a common vision. From humble beginnings, we have grown into a respected brand in the fitness industry. Just like Gymshark's garage mentality, we started with a dream and the determination to make high-quality activewear and fitness equipment accessible to all. Our founders' dedication to excellence and innovation continues to drive our success.

Our Offerings

At Fitness Freaks, we are committed to providing you with the tools you need to be your personal best. Our range of products includes comfortable workout clothing, durable accessories, and state-of-the-art fitness equipment. We understand that sweat is your sport, and we aim to support you with the clothing you'll sweat in, the content that inspires you, and a community that helps you be your best.

Our Community

Although we are a new and growing community, we are passionate about building a network of fitness enthusiasts who share our dedication to a healthy and active lifestyle. Our journey has just begun, and we are excited to have you as part of our community. Our heart and soul are in Melbourne, where we are headquartered and continue to grow.

Our Core Values

Our values are the foundation of our identity and success:

  • Be human: We are accessible, inclusive, and humble.
  • Give a shit: We care for the world, those around us, and actively seek positive change.
  • Do the right thing: We are honest, trustworthy, and genuine.
  • Find the Fitness Freaks way: We embrace ambition, agility, and disruption.
  • Put family first: We prioritize our community unconditionally.
